Local Resources

Below is listed common resources that can be found in any town or city you may live in. Please note that it's NOT guaranteed. You'll need to contact these resources to ensure that they can provide the help that you seek.

  • Hospitals: Offers CPR classes and have connections to other resources
  • Support Groups: There are a numerous amount of places that host group meetings like Fitness Centers, Community Centers, schools, clinics, etc.
  • YMCA or other Fitness Centers: Exercising alone can sometimes help mentally
  • Homelessness: Depending on the area you live in, there are places that'll take you in like churches and shelters
  • WIC, Medicaid and Food and Nutrition: Go to government site for the number in your state
